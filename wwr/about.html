Manie's Rafting Camp

Welcome to our rafting family. We specialize in providing unforgettable white water adventures for thrill-seekers of all skill levels, guided by certified professionals who prioritize your safety and excitement.

History

Founded over two decades ago by a group of passionate river guides, our company grew from a single raft into a premier adventure destination. We have spent years exploring channels, mastering currents, and sharing our love for the wild outdoors with thousands of happy adventurers.
